Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Qwen-Image collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI image generation platform, including qwen image comfyui integration, WAN2.2 features, and qwen image gguf models.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When using Qwen-Image services, we may collect:

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Usage data from qwen image comfyui workflows
  • Generated image metadata
  • Model interaction logs (WAN2.2 usage patterns)

1.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP addresses and location data
  • Qwen image gguf model download statistics
  • Performance metrics for AI image generation

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Enable qwen image comfyui integration and gguf model access
  • Improve AI Models: Enhance WAN2.2 capabilities and text rendering quality
  • User Support: Assist with technical issues and feature requests
  • Security: Protect against abuse and maintain platform integrity
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns for qwen image generation

3. Data Protection for AI Models

3.1 Qwen Image GGUF Models

When you download or use qwen image gguf models:

  • Model usage is logged for security and improvement purposes
  • Generated outputs are not stored unless explicitly saved by you
  • Model weights and parameters remain proprietary and protected

3.2 ComfyUI Integration Privacy

For qwen image comfyui workflows:

  • Workflow configurations may be cached locally
  • Node connections and parameters are not transmitted to our servers
  • Generated images remain in your local ComfyUI environment

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal information or AI-generated content to third parties.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect rights and safety
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

5. AI Content and Intellectual Property

Regarding content created with Qwen-Image:

  • Your Prompts: Text prompts you input remain private
  • Generated Images: You retain rights to images you create
  • Model Training: Generated content is not used to train future models without consent
  • WAN2.2 Outputs: Enhanced features maintain same privacy protections

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures:

  • Encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Secure access controls for qwen image gguf model distribution
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Isolated processing for AI image generation requests

7. International Data Transfers

Qwen-Image operates globally. When you use our services:

  • Data may be processed in multiple jurisdictions
  • We ensure adequate protection for international transfers
  • Qwen image comfyui integrations respect local data laws
  • Model downloads via gguf format follow regional compliance requirements

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your generated content and settings
  • Opt-out: Disable certain data collection features

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences for qwen image generation
  • Analyze usage patterns for WAN2.2 features
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized recommendations for AI workflows

10. Children's Privacy

Qwen-Image is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as we enhance Qwen-Image with new features like improved qwen image comfyui integration or additional gguf model formats. We will notify users of significant changes through our platform.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Address: Alibaba DAMO Academy Privacy Office
